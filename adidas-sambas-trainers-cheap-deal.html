Im actually crying' fashionistas say after discovering nationwide store selling Adidas Sambas

IT'S the pair of trainers that's taken social media and influencer feeds by storm - the viral Adidas Sambas.

But before you dash to the high street to bag the trendy footwear, money-saving fashionistas have revealed where you can get them with a hefty discount.

Instead of forking out a fortune for the classic trainers that come in a range of styles, one cash-savvy style lover urged to check out a less-known shop that offers huge savings - The Original Factory Shop.

The discount department store chain, which was established in 1969, is the best pick for those looking to shop big brands for less, with up to 70 per cent off the real retail price (RRP).

According to their website, there is something for everyone and every need - whether you're after a cheap DIY project for your home, beauty bargains, towels, electrics, toys, bits for your garden and of course, fashion.

Here, you can pick between a number of well-known brands, including posh designer Hugo Boss, Jimmy Choo, Nike, Calvin Klein, as well as Russel Hobbs and Adidas.

Those after the viral Adidas Sambas will be pleased to hear that you can now get them for £60, which is almost 50 per cent less than in other stores.

According to the shopper, only known as @callme_ge2000 on TikTok, she had spotted the bargain deal too late, as she had already purchased a new pair for ''over £100'' a month ago.

There is also plenty of colour schemes to pick from, such as blue and red, black and white, and navy and baby blue.

''I will be going back for ones blue ones 100%,'' added the fashionista, who claimed she was ''crying''.

The Original Factory Shop has around 200 stores around the nation and you can find the nearest location with their Store Finder.

Dozens of fellow cash-savvy fashion lovers raced to comments to tag their pals and family.

One cried: ''Omg I miss the Factory Shop.''

Some shared where you can bag the trainers for even less, with one revealing: ''£43 in Sports Direct.''

Someone else chimed in with another alternative, writing: ''Should also shop on Wynsors.com for great price Adidas originals.''

If you're looking to replenish your footwear collection on the cheap, make sure to also check out the latest deals to land in Primark - here, you can bag stylish boots for as little as £6.

One such stylish option is a pair of basic black boots with a chunky platform - a trend that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

These, the store shared in their video, are now going from £20 to £10, making them the ultimate bargain you should definitely not sleep on.
