Aldi unveils 'matching' fashion essentials range starting at $9.99 - it's giving spring vibes

ALDI has unveiled a cute new matching fashion item starting for under $10.

The retailer says the clothes give “spring vibes,” and are perfect for families.

Aldi has released a new line of women’s summer dresses with matching mini-me girls’ dresses.

The store announced the new product last week with a tweet, writing: “Spring vibes only.”

The Serra ladies’ maxi dress is a sleeveless summer piece that features a high neckline and fits down to the floor.

It is available in three designs - a green floral print, a navy floral print, and a solid dusky pink.

Read more about Aldi

The women’s dresses are $12.99.

However, all three styles come in matching girls’ dresses.

The Lily & Dan Girls’ Maxi Dresses are $9.99.

Both women’s and girls’ dresses can be found in the Aldi Finds aisle of your local store, although availability will vary by location.

Aldi is best known for its low prices on groceries and home products, but the store had a major fashion hit last year.

A $12.99 wrap dress, also from the Serra brand, went viral online.

Some Aldi fans traveled hours to find one as the store’s reputation for fashion skyrocketed.

Speaking on Good Morning America, Passionate Penny Pincher blogger Jamie Miles explained: "Aldi fashion tends to be super figure friendly.

“So it's not only geared toward one specific body type.

“Everybody can pull off the cardigans, the sweatshirt dresses, the little rain booties, but also the price is crazy affordable.”

However, like many Aldi Finds products, these fashion items are often available for a limited time only.

This scarcity can drive demand up even further, especially when shoppers buy more than one of the same item.
