Alex Scott's most daring outfits, from going braless in see-through dress to risque fashion shoot

ALEX SCOTT leads a glamorous lifestyle and can regularly be seen on a red carpet or starring in a photoshoot.

The former England Women's star has become a household name thanks to her presenting skills, which has seen her host Football Focus and The One Show.

Scott first dazzled during her appearance on Strictly Come Dancing in 2019, where she finished fifth.

Since then she has appeared in fashion shoots showing off her flawless body in risque outfits.

The former Arsenal defender began dating singer Jess Glynne this year and the couple shared their first public kiss earlier this month.

The pair attended the Attitude Awards together where Scott stunned on the red carpet.

Leathered up

Braless bravery

Scott hit the red carpet at the GQ Men of the Year awards and stole the show in a see-through dress.

The outfit certainly left nothing to the imagination in a silver glitzy floor-length maxi dress.

Queen Akasha

Scott went as her favourite character from The Vampire Chronicles for Halloween and pulled off the look perfectly.

She showed off her stunning abs in a costume that looked like it took hours of effort to put together.

Killer clown

Her make-up and red afro wig meant she looked the part.
