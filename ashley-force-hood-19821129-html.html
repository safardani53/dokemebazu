She is married to Daniel Hood, who works for John Force Racing. She interrupted the race in 2011 as the couple were expecting their first child. Since their child was born, Force Hood announced his retirement from playing. Force was named the Rookie of the NHRA POWERade Tractor Series in November 2007.
