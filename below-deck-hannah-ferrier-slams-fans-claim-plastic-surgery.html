BELOW Deck Mediterranean alum Hannah Ferrier slammed fans who claim she got plastic surgery.

The 34-year-old insisted she just “gained weight” in her face after welcoming her baby.

On Wednesday, the Bravo alum took to Instagram to post a selfie.

She captioned the snap: “It doesn’t happen very often these days but I still love feeling like a woman every now and then instead of just a mum! 💗”

After fans took to the comments section to accuse Hannah of getting plastic surgery due to her fuller looking face, the TV personality clapped back as she explained she’s gained weight.

One person commented: “Yikes! I love Hannah, but what has happened.”

Hannah replied: “I had a baby and out on weight so my face has changed - as has my a**! That’s life hey?!”

As others accused the Below Deck alum of having plastic surgery, she responded: “How f**ked is it?!?!

“I’ve put on weight due to a baby and an injury that occurred during birth and now I’m disgusting and have had so much work done!!!!”

She then told another critic: “I’ve never had fillers in my life.”

Hannah shared that she gave birth to her first child in an Instagram post in November.

Alongside three photos of her newborn daughter that she shares with her Josh Roberts, Hannah wrote: "Madam has arrived! Josh and I are so happy to welcome to the world our little girl - Ava Grace Roberts. Born on the 26th of October."

The new mom added: "She is already a little menace that has captured our hearts forever."

One month after welcoming Ava, Hannah revealed that she became engaged to her longtime boyfriend.

Alongside a selfie that showed Hannah flaunting the diamond ring, the former Below Deck star wrote: "And then there were three...and a ring 💍🥂"

Hannah and Josh have been together for over two years, while they moved into a new home together in Australia around the time they welcomed their daughter.
