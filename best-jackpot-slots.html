    • What types of jackpot slots are there in UK casinos?

    There is not just a single type of jackpot slot available in the UK today. Below, we outline the different versions that await players at our recommended casinos:

    Local jackpots

    With this type of slot, the progressive jackpot is specific to just a single game. This is in contrast to some other games, where the money is pooled from multiple titles. As a result, the local jackpot is contributed to by fewer players, while there is also less competition for places. 

    Pooled jackpots

    With pooled jackpot slots, bets placed on different casino sites add to the total prize. As such, potentially millions of players can add to the jackpot. So, when you view the jackpot amount online, this will be the same as on other sites. 

    Fixed jackpots

    The most common type of jackpot slot is fixed. Here, players can win a set amount of money. The jackpot does not change, meaning that it will be the same today as it was yesterday. Fixed jackpot slots are available with the majority of online casinos in the UK. 

    Progressive jackpots

    Many players are attracted to progressive jackpot slots. This is because the maximum win fluctuates over time. So, the more bets are placed, the greater the jackpot will be. However, once the jackpot has been won, it resets to its original amount. 

    Multiple jackpot slots

    With such slots, there is more than one jackpot up for grabs. In some cases, you will need to trigger bonus rounds in order to be in with a chance of winning additional jackpots. Bonus wheels are one of the common methods used by developers. 

    How do jackpot slots work?

    Jackpot slots are different to regular slots. In most cases, the jackpot is progressive. This means that every time a bet is placed by a player, the jackpot grows. As such, the jackpot is not fixed. Jackpots can be worth millions. However, the jackpot will return to its default amount following a win, before building again over time. It is possible that a jackpot can extend over a network, meaning that the amount available to win is the same with multiple casinos. As such, the jackpot could be won by someone playing on a different site than you. 

    Are jackpot slots rigged?

    No. As is the case with other software-based online casino games, jackpot slots use a Random Number Generator (RNG) to determine the outcome of each spin. As such, you can place bets safe in the knowledge that you stand a fair chance of winning. 

    Are progressive jackpots and jackpot slots the same?

    No. With a progressive jackpot slot, a proportion of each bet goes towards the total jackpot. Meanwhile, when the jackpot is won, it reverts back to the original amount. Meanwhile, traditional jackpot slots just have a jackpot to be won, which will often be smaller in size.
