Cath Kidston's Alice In Wonderland has finally arrived so heres the first look

CATH Kidston has introduced it's magical new Alice in Wonderland range in a beautiful collection that features Alice and friends amongst the designer's classic floral prints.

The range includes clothes, accessories and bags as well as crockery and plant pots so the whole home can be covered in the animated characters.

Other than Alice, popular characters including the Cheshire Cat, Tweedledum and Tweedledee, the White Rabbit, Caterpillar and the Queen of Hearts also appear.

According to the official Cath Kidston blog, the range combines archive and original Disney drawings with their own iconic designs.

It says: "We were inspired by the scene in the original 1951 film where the white roses are being painted red by the playing cards, and we immediately though of our archive filled with roses."

You won't have to go down the rabbit hole to access the collection though, as they will be available online from May 24th, and in all UK stores from May 25th.

There are a selection of bags in the range, including a delicate tote that pictures Alice in a field of daisy's and a more practical royal blue shoulder bag with a white print of the Cheshire Cat.

Backpacks for children are also in the collection, one printed with Tweedledum and Tweedledee for boys, and the other with a large heart, flowers and Alice for girls.

Two women's dresses have so far been revealed, including one that matches the blue tote bag, and another summer dress which features quotes from the film.

A matching girls dress, with a red sash around the mid, and a polka dotted apron also have been teased by the designer.

Fans flooded to the Facebook to celebrate the collection, and one wrote: "I can't wait from this! Nor can my 11-year-old daughter!"

Another gushed: "This is the most amazing news ever! I have to have something from this collection!"
