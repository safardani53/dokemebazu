Career

Choi Youngjae has always enjoyed singing and aspired to be a singer since he was a child. As a result, he enrolled in a music academy in his hometown of Mokpo in order to become a more professional singer. In 2011, he received the Vocal Excellence Award at the Mokpo Youth Music Festival.

Then, in 2013, Youngjae was accepted as a trainee in JYP Entertainment after a close audition in the academy. Youngjae made his debut as a singer after a seven-month training period. In January 2014, he made his debut as the main vocalist and dancer of the South Korean boy band Got7 with ‘Girls Girls Girls.’ He was the team’s last member to be chosen.

In addition, Youngjae and his bandmates made their acting debut with their web drama ‘Dream Knight.’

Youngjae has named himself ‘Ars’ as a producer, which stands for Ars longa vita brevis, which means Art is long and life is short. As a result, the majority of his works are credited to him as ‘Ars.’ He contributed to his team’s album by composing, writing lyrics, and producing.
