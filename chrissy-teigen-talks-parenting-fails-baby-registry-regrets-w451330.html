Chrissy Teigen Talks Parenting Fails, Baby Registry Regrets

Chrissy Teigen has come a long way since her daughter Luna’s arrival back in April. “I remember when I was breast-feeding I would drop certain things on her head while I was eating them,” the Cravings cookbook author told Us Weekly Video in the video above. “Nothing, like, hot or dangerous or anything, but it would always just give me the biggest chuckle to see a little guacamole be like, ‘bloop!’” 

The 30-year-old Sports Illustrated model, who is married to R&B singer John Legend, also dished post-baby relationship advice. “I think the worst thing that can happen when you have a child is, like, grow this slow hatred or resentment because [the man] is not able to do very much, and that turns into other things,” Teigen admitted. “You get mad when they want to go out. You are breast-feeding. You are pumping. You are like … ‘I’m stuck home with the [baby.]’ You can’t be like that. There are just things that they can’t do. They want to help but they just can’t.” 

Watch the candid video above to hear Teigen discuss her baby registry regrets and must-haves, whether she or John will be the stricter parent and the most surprising thing about motherhood. 

Teigen and her husband welcomed their first child together on April 14 following a painful struggle with infertility. The famous duo — who have been married for three years — have been open about their desire to have more kids.
