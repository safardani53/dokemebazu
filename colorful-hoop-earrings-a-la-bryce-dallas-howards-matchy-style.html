Colorful Hoop Earrings a la Bryce Dallas Howards Matchy Style

We’ve heard the idea of matching your handbag to your shoes, but Bryce Dallas Howard just upped the matchy-matchy style game by rocking a pair of hoop earrings that were almost the exact same color as her pretty jade green eyes. The actress, who is currently promoting her new film Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom, shared a pic of herself rocking the color-coordinated trend on Instagram in honor of #NationalSelfieDay on Thursday, June 21. She captioned the snap “dino green,” and while the earrings’ pastel hue certainly does call to mind her reptilian co-stars in the flick, they also make her gorgeous peepers pop.

We deemed hoop earrings the trend of the summer when Tracee Ellis Ross rocked an oversized white pair last month that was the epitome of warm-weather chic, and now Bryce is making the case for embracing colorful ear candy that can enhance your natural features — and the results are truly eye-catching.
