Who is Danna Bui-Negrete from RHOSLC?

THE REAL Housewives of Salt Lake City is back with a season 3 reunion set to premiere Wednesday, January 25, 2023.

Among the newest housewives was Danna Bui-Negrete, and fans want to know more about her.

Who is Danna Bui-Negrete from RHOSLC?

Outside of her role on RHOSLC, Bui-Negrete is known as a successful entrepreneur and real estate agent.

Despite having a high-profile career, little information is available on her personal life as she tends to stay out of the spotlight. However, she is sure to add to the group's drama this season.

Her Bravo biography states that she gets "tangled in the group’s drama" after bringing a "rumor about Jen [Shah] to light."

When Bui-Negrete is not working, she can often be found posting about her life on Instagram to thousands of followers.

Since starting her real estate career, she has been recognized several times and was even listed as one of Salt Lake City’s Top 500 Realtors.

According to her Instagram page, she often sells homes worth hundreds of thousands of dollars, helping add to her fortune.

However, details on her exact net worth are unclear at this time.

Is Danna Bui-Negrete married?

Bui-Negrete is a busy person due to her career and new reality television fame, but that does not stop her from spending time with her family.

Along with being a successful real estate agent and entrepreneur, she is also a wife and mother.

She is currently married to Ernesto Negrete, who works as a human resources leader at Sterling ATM, according to his LinkedIn page.

Prior to that, he obtained his bachelor's degree in communication from Westminster College.

Over the years, the celebrity couple has welcomed three girls.

Negrete is also the father of Aliyah Negrete, who he welcomed during a previous relationship.

While Bui-Negrete tends to keep personal information about her family out of the spotlight, they often make appearances on her social media pages.
