How many tattoos does Demi Lovato have?

TIME and time again, superstar Demi Lovato shares their tattoo compilation with their fans on social media.

Demi added another tattoo to their collection of body art, which the performer revealed on their Instagram page.

How many tattoos does Demi Lovato have?

Since Demi's rise to fame, the singer acquired over 25 known tattoos.

One of Lovato's most notable pieces of body art is a word illustration on their neck that reads "survivor."

Another design the singer has is a lion's face on the back of their hand, which represents their zodiac sign, as well as their song Lionheart.

On the performer's opposite hand, reads another fully inked design that reads: "Love will live forever in an infinite universe."

The meaning behind the word filled tattoo are lyrics to one of Lovato's favorite songs, Infinite Universe by Beautiful Chorus.

Celebrity tattoo artist, Dr. Woo, is responsible for a few of Demi Lovato's mentioned designs, including their spider illustration.

Demi Lovato and Dr. Woo put Demi's then-new tattoo on display on both of their Instagram stories on January 8, 2022.

Dr. Woo designed the illustration of a large spider onto the side of Lovato's head.

The former Disney Channel star explained their tattoo choice, as they wrote on Instagram: "It was grandmother spider who taught us many things."

Demi continued: "She taught us about pottery and weaving."

They went on to say: "She taught us about fire and light and dark."

"She taught us that we are all connected on the web - each one of us having our place in this world," the singer concluded on Instagram.

Demi finished off their message with a black Emoji love heart and a spider icon.

Prior to the spider body ink reveal, Demi liked one of Dr. Woo's then-latest tattoo designs on Instagram - which saw a huge spider inked onto the back of a man's skull - spanning from the back of the neck to the crown of the head.

Many fans believed this is where Demi's inspiration came from, with one commenting: "We know," with several eye emojis.

Another directly quizzed: "Was this your inspo?" as a third commented, "@ddlovato love you so much."

Did Demi Lovato get a new tattoo?

On March 30, 2022, the Camp Rock alum shared the news of another piece of body art with their followers.

On Instagram, the singer posted a photo of their forearm tattoo that read 'Choose Love.'

They captioned the revealing snapshot: "Choose Love - always. I’m inspired by the work @chooselove and their partners are doing to support refugees around the world, including those fleeing Ukraine."

Demi mentioned the refugee fundraising organization, Choose Love, and said: "Take action with @chooselove and enter for a chance to win time with me in the studio to hear new music. Head to my link in bio to enter."

In the post, they tagged the Ukrainian tattoo artist responsible and said: "Thank you, @gusak_tattoo it was such an honor learning about your home country."

The pop singer disclosed that if fans donate to Choose Love, they have the chance to win studio time with Demi - which they will then use to preview new music with the lucky fan.

Was Demi Lovato in rehab?

Demi Lovato had a near-fatal overdose from fentanyl in 2018.

The performer spoke about their reasons for the lifestyle change in an interview on CBS Sunday Morning in 2021.

"I’ve learned that it doesn’t work for me to say that I’m never going to do this again,” Lovato said in their docuseries, Dancing with the Devil.

They added that they’re “done with the stuff that’s going to kill (them).”

Lovato stated: "Recovery isn’t a one-size-fits-all solution. You shouldn’t be forced to get sober if you’re not ready."

They also said: "You shouldn’t get sober for other people. You have to do it for yourself."
