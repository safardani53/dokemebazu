CHRISTMAS is not too far and Dollar Tree has great deals and decor to make your home holiday-ready.

All the festive items at Dollar Tree are only $1.25, so don't miss out and see what gems you can pick up.

There were a few Thanksgiving items left, but Dollar Tree is currently filled with Christmas trees, ornaments, jingle bells, and more.

A TikTok influencer, peachyymaddi, revealed the top 10 categories she found at Dollar Tree that she thinks are worth buying for the holidays.

All the items Maddi found were only $1.25.

Availability will vary by store.

1. Pie recipe signs

"I was pleasantly surprised to be greeted by these pie recipes once I walked in. This is such a steal," Maddi said.

There were four different pie recipes Maddi found - pumpkin, cherry, pecan, and apple pie.

2. Thanksgiving-themed hanging signs

There were also more options for hanging signs.

There were individual pie slice wall art that's perfect for Thanksgiving or the fall season too.

Each sign stated: "Give thanks," or "Gather."

3. Christmas towel

Enough with the Thanksgiving decor, Christmas is around the corner, and the first item Maddi found is a Christmas-themed towel.

The towel wrote: " North Pole. Bed & Breakfast."

Again, the item is only $1.25.

4. Christmas signs

Wall art or anything on a wall can instantly change the energy of the environment, and one of Maddi's favorites is Christmas signs.

"Honey I am here for it. I am a Christmas baby. They had some decorative signs," Maddi said.

Some signs wrote: "Merry Bright," "Sleigh rides," or "Hot cocoa."

5. Mini trees

Dollar Tree also sold miniature Christmas trees for $1.25.

It is 18 inches tall with 35 fold-out tips, they are the perfect size to stand on a table or counter.

It also comes in two colors - white and green.

The trees come packaged individually, and you can decorate them with ornaments and lights.

6. Jingle bells

Maddi also found metal bell door hangers, which came in three different colors.

One is a beige one, another is a red and black plaid, and the third color is a white and black plaid.

The bell door hangers came in gold, red and white.

7. Tree topper

"Here's a quick bougie on a budget tip, get your tree topper at Dollar Tree," Maddi recommended.

The tree toppers came in gold and silver - colorful and shiny, the star-shaped tree toppers were intricately designed.

They also have a sparkly metallic finish and swirling design.

8. Christmas village collection

For people who want to decorate more intricately, there were more finds Maddi recommended.

There were tiny Christmas trees, gates, houses, and more by Cobblestone Corner.

It's all the pieces to make a village, costing $1.25 a piece.

9. Mini Christmas tree ornaments

"They have all of the ornaments that you would need. This is perfect if you're in college or if you're just living alone," Maddi said.

From snowflakes to bells to miniature lights, there were several options, and it's all for $1.25 each.

10. Mugs

Last, but not least, Dollar tree had oversized mugs that would be perfect for baking.

Mug cakes are easy and simple to follow, and it takes a few minutes to prepare.

These mugs are actually ceramic soup mugs with coffee-mug-style handles.

They come in three colors - red, white and black.

Tips for saving this holiday season

It's no doubt that at the end of the year, people tend to spend more money with holidays following one after another.

But there are ways to spend less and save more money.

For example, do your shopping during Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales.

Black Friday starts for Walmart on November 7 at 7pm ET online and on November 9 in stores. New deals are released every Monday.
