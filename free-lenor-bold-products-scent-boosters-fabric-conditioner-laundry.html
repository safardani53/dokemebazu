How to nab FREE laundry goodies - including Lenor scent boosters and fabric conditioner

A SAVVY shopper has shared how she nabbed Lenor and Bold laundry products for FREE. 

The two cleaning brands have teamed up to create a Wellbeing Dream Team of products - but not everyone has to pay for them.

Among these is Lenor scent boosters and fabric conditioner in its new Cherry Blossom & Rose Water fragrance. 

Bold has also dropped laundry pods with Lenor’s new fabric conditioner built in - meaning your laundry will be oozing with a cherry blossom aroma. 

Hristina Galabova and a few other lucky shoppers have managed to nab the three-product set for FREE. 

“It's been a month now being part of the Wellbeing Dream Team,” she posted on Facebook.

The freebies are thanks to a little-known “online community” called SuperSavvyMe.

Bosses of the organisation say it “helps people get more out of life” by sending free products for them to try and share their views on. 

“If you love testing out new products and sharing honest reviews with friends and family, you’ll love what we have to offer,” they said. 

You can land some freebies by downloading the SuperSavvy App and earning points to win products and prizes. 

However, you don’t typically get to choose what you get freebies - brands will send you packages to review as and when. 

A whopping 7.5k people were chosen through SuperSavvyMe to review the new Lenor and Bold laundry products. 

Each person received a box of the brand-new Bold Platinum PODS Washing Capsules, a bottle of the brand-new Lenor Fabric Softener with essential oils and a tube of Lenor In-Wash Scent Boosters. 

As well as the Cherry Blossom & Rose Water scent, there was also a Citrus & White Verbena fragrance up for grabs.

“The fragrance and laundry care experts at Bold and Lenor believe that when your laundry smells good, you feel good,” a blurb on the project reads.
