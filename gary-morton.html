Who is Gary Morton:

Gary Morton is a famous Comedian. He was born on December 19, 1924 and his birthplace is The Bronx, NY. Gary is also well known as, Borscht Belt comedian who married Lucille Ball and became her warm-up comic on The Lucy Show. He executive produced her shows Here’s Lucy and Life With Lucy.

Gary is originated from United States. He parodied comedian Milton Berle in the 1974 film Lenny, which was one of his few film roles.
