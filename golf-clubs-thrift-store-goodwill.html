A POPULAR thrift store shopper has snagged a name-brand golf club set for just $3 per club.

The bargain hunter and TikToker (@SurfsUpFinds) resells many items he finds at thrift stores, storage units, and garage sales.

A self-proscribed educator and business owner, the TikToker shares his favorite finds and teaches others how to spot valuable items.

He recently posted a Tiktok where shared with his 19,400 followers some of his latest purchases from Goodwill. 

He bought them all for just $2.99 apiece.

“I hardly ever find good golf clubs - and a full set,” he said.

The brand he found was called Cleveland, a longtime golf club maker where equipment sells anywhere between $85.71 to $800 per club.

The bargain hunter revealed that those same clubs have sold on eBay anywhere from $120 up to $185.

NIFTY THRIFTING

While thrifting can be a tough task, it doesn't need to be.

It's wise to give yourself a clear goal before going on a shopping haul. Decide what you'd like to buy, and stick to a plan.

It could be helpful to take note of the things you already have, then a list of the things you’re looking for to avoid impulse buys.

Next up is to check when each local store restocks.

Most stores will likely be busiest on the weekend, and many of the popular items may be gone.

Lastly, learn how to discern quality.

This may take some research as many stores offer designer brands or vintage items.

Learning about quality materials and specific brands before buying may be helpful.
