Hunter Johansson is Scarlett Johansson's Twin Brother

Hunter Johansson, Scarlett Johansson‘s twin brother, is younger than his sister by a whopping three minutes.

Named as one of People magazine’s “Hot Bachelors” in their “Single and Sexy Men of 2008” feature, Scarjo’s twin seeks dating advice from his “big sister.”

“She tells me how girls think. When I’m stuck, I call,” Hunter told People. “She’s set me up on dates. It wasn’t with anybody famous. It was with some school friends – all very beautiful, wonderful girl.”

Hunter left his job as a community organizer at Manhattan Borough President Scott Stringer’s office in June 2008, to become the campaign organizer for Barack Obama in Denver.

The announcement came days after Scarlett said in an interview that she e-mails with Obama on a regular basis, leaving many wondering if she helped her brother get the job. According to Scarlett’s publicists, it was in fact Hunter who introduced his sister to Obama years ago.

New Yorkers who have met him say he is polite, a Mets fan, a good-looking guy who wears jeans and a T-shirt off duty and often uses words like “dude,” “dawg” and “bro.”
