My husband has always cross-dressed - but now I fear he wants a sex change

DEAR DEIDRE: MY husband of 20 years loves dressing up as a woman.

I’ve known this from very early on in our relationship and because I love him so much and want him to be happy I have ­supported him.

But now I’m terrified his needs go much deeper and he really wants to transition into a woman. I fear if he did do this, I wouldn’t be able to stay with him.

I’m 41 and he’s 44, we have a 19-year-old daughter and an 18-year-old son who are both away at college.

Now he wants to dress up the whole time and stays up late editing photos to make himself look more feminine.

He comes to bed so late even though he gets up at 6am to start his work as a train driver.

We still have a good sex life and he’s told me he is only attracted to women.

I want him to be happy and can’t help but feel he is hiding something more.

DEIDRE SAYS: Wanting to wear women’s clothes is a very different thing from actually wanting to be a woman.

Having a sex change is a huge procedure and if he is considering this then it could take him time to feel absolutely certain.

Most cross-dressers are very happy in their heterosexual relationships.

It’s the dressing up that fulfils their needs.

I am sending my support pack Cross-dressing, which gives more detail.
