ITV in last minute schedule shake up with new show Mamma Mia! I Have A Dream

ITV has been caught up in a last minute schedule shakeup with new show, Mamma Mia! I Have A Dream.

The eight-part competition programme sees hopefuls compete to become musical theatre super troupers.

The show sends out a nationwide search to find the future musical theatre stars of global smash hit and feel-good musical Mamma Mia!.

But in case anyone missed the first episode on Sunday - which had 3.7 million viewers - ITV has decided to repeat it this Saturday as well.

It will air just before the Rugby World Cup Final at 5.30pm, and will overlap with Strictly Come Dancing on BBC One by 15 minutes.

The previously scheduled Harry Potter film has been pushed back until Sunday afternoon to make room for the competition series.
