Right-handed starter Jack Flaherty was born in Burbank, California, on October 15, 1995. He has primarily remained a free agent and is now in an agreement with the Detroit Tigers. 

He was picked in the first round of the 2014 MLB draft by the St. Louis Cardinals and made his Major League Baseball (MLB) debut with them in 2017. Jack has also been a member of the Baltimore Orioles.

Flaherty went to Harvard-Westlake School in Los Angeles. He pitched and played shortstop and third base for the Harvard-Westlake varsity baseball team for four years.

Despite signing a letter of intent to attend the University of North Carolina, Flaherty began his professional career after the Cardinals selected him in the first round, 34th overall, in the 2014 MLB draft.

Flaherty was promoted to the major leagues on September 1, 2017, and debuted against the San Francisco Giants.
