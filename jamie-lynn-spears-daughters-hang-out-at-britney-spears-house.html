Jamie Lynn Spears, Daughters Hang Out at Britney Spears House

Fun in the sun! Jamie Lynn Spears made the journey from Louisiana to California with her daughters, Maddie and Ivey, for a visit to their aunt Britney Spears’ house.

The Zoey 101 alum, 28, shared two photos on her Instagram Stories on Wednesday, June 5, of her family hanging out in the backyard of the Princess of Pop’s Thousand Oaks mansion. In the first snap, Maddie, 10, held Ivey, 13 months, as they wore swimsuits and struck a pose by an in-ground swimming pool. Britney, 37, could be seen walking in an aqua-colored tank top and matching gym shorts in the background.

The second picture showed Maddie, whom Jamie Lynn shares with ex-fiancé Casey Aldridge, on a trampoline with Britney’s son Preston, 13, while the All That alum’s husband, Jamie Watson, watched and held their daughter, Ivey, nearby.

Jamie Lynn made headlines in 2007 when she announced that she was pregnant at the age of 16. After splitting from Aldridge, now 30, three years later, she found love with Watson, whom she wed in 2014.

Britney, for her part, has been spending a lot of time with her family since completing 30 days of treatment at a mental health facility in April. Us Weekly exclusively revealed that the Grammy winner attended her son Preston’s eighth grade graduation ceremony on May 29. Her youngest son, Jayden, 12, attends the same private Christian school in Los Angeles County, where they both play tackle football.
