JLo, 54, reveals her chiseled abs in just a bra top and pink skirt on St Barts night without husband

JENNIFER Lopez has shown off her incredibly toned figure in a tiny outfit while on her most recent vacation.

The 54-year-old actress was spotted alone in St. Barthelemy without the accompaniment of her husband Ben Affleck, 51.

JLo was seen rocking a high-waisted, pink maxi skirt with ruffled tiers as she shopped around Gustavia.

Showing off her chiseled abs, the singer paired the skirt with a tiny white crop top that tied behind her neck.

The Marry Me actress finalized her trendy vacation look with a pair of oversized sunglasses and a mini Chanel bag that fell below her hips.

JLo had her shaggy, highlighted locks left down and naturally wavy for her day on the town.

The new solo shots come shortly after reports of tension between them behind the scenes were made earlier this year.

Sources claimed Ben was growing tired of JLo’s diva demands for everything to be “perfect” in the quest to find their dream home, while they were also been pictured arguing at red-carpet dos as he took issue with her revealing tops.

Tying the knot in Last Vegas last July seemed to be a fairytale ending for the couple — nicknamed Bennifer — who had called off their first engagement 20 years earlier.

But recovering alcoholic Ben is said to prefer a more relaxed approach to life than the businesslike singer.

Earlier this month, the two seemed to put any differences aside as they dressed to impress at Nya Studios for Elle's Women in Hollywood Celebration.

JLo's bespoke outfit looked like she'd been covered in liquid metal and stopped abruptly just above her chest.

The 54-year-old beauty wore her hair scraped back and dangly earrings capped off her eye-catching outfit.

The couple wrapped an arm around each other as they posed for photos before JLo stepped away and modeled her head-turning outfit from multiple angles.

Despite Ben's issues with JLo's revealing outfits, she recently told Vogue that she is very much in control of every aspect of her life, including her fashion choices.

“I’m finally at the point in my life where I love every part of myself unapologetically," she explained to the publication.

"Every part of me, my body, my voice, my choices, even, like, my mistakes. All of it made me who I am and got me to where I am today.

"Ben wants me to understand my worth and know my value. I feel even more relaxed and comfortable, which makes me feel even more beautiful than I have ever felt with someone else.”
