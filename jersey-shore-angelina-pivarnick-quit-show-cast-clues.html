Jersey Shore fans think Angelina Pivarnick has QUIT the show as cast drop clues after her divo

JERSEY Shore fans think Angelina Pivarnick may have quit the show as she is missing from yet another cast photo.

The 35-year-old failed to make an appearance in Deena Cortese's snap with her costars Jenni "JWoww" Farley and Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi on Tuesday.

In the photo, Deena, JWoww and Snooki all wore cowboy hats while sitting in the backseat of a car.

Deena captioned it: "12 years ago we met super young.

"Now we’re grown women with beautiful families, growing businesses and still making TV together.

"Happy #internationalwomensday to these two. Love you b***hes."

Jersey Shore fans shared the photo on a Reddit thread, claiming it's further evidence that Angelina may have left the show.

One wrote: "Is Angelina still on the show? Based on the cast members' Instagrams, they’re filming but no sign of Ange."

They also pointed out that Angelina has removed any mention of Jersey Shore from their Instagram bio section.

Another posted: "With all the recent drama the girls are probably only willing to film separate from Angelina."

A third speculated: "Maybe she needed a time out. Her divorce is probably is getting nasty & she got some work done. I don't think Angelina would let the JS go that easily."

Over the weekend, Angelina was missing once again from a group photo of the cast.

JWoww, 37, shared a snap of her, Snooki, 34, and Deena, 34, filming on a private jet with a cameraman behind them.

It's unclear why Angelina wasn't with her castmates, although the reality star has been having a turbulent time as of late.

SHORE-LY A SCANDAL

Her husband Chris Larangeira, 44, filed for divorce back in January.

Chris stated that he and Angelina had “suffered irreconcilable differences which have caused a breakdown of the marriage for at least a period of six months.”  

According to the New Jersey family court clerk, Angelina has yet to file her own response to Chris' divorce complaint.

The Sun reported last week that Angelina cheated on Chris with Acapulo Shore's Luis "Potro" Caballero.

According to insiders, she hooked up with the Mexican reality star, 29, while they were both filming an international show in Spain.

Angelina flew to Spain before Christmas to film the new Jersey Shore spinoff show - Paramount Plus' All Star Shore - and promptly met Luis during the wild taping, sources claimed.

AFFAIR CLAIMS

After the star returned home to New Jersey, Chris allegedly found out that Angelina had cheated on him with co-star Luis and immediately moved out.

As The Sun first reported, Angelina had previously had a steamy affair with New Jersey native Joe Tarallo, 33, which Chris allegedly already knew about.

But the new cheating claims pushed Chris over the edge, the source insisted.

"He had known about Joe from Old Bridge, but thought she was now loyal and dedicated to working on their marriage,” a source close to the star said.
