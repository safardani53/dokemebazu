John Hagee Biography

John Hagee (age 82) was born on 12 April 1940 in Goose Creek, Texas, USA.  John Hagee is the CEO of Global Evangelism Television and he also owns John Hagee Ministries too. He is also a well-known senior pastor in Cornerstone Church. He has completed his Bachelor's from Trinity University and Master's Degree from the University of North Texas. He is Christian Religious, American nationality, and his ethnicity is White.
