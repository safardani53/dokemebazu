Kardashian-Jenner Christmas dresses ranked Kims dripping diamonds to Trues tutu

The Kardashians go all out for their annual Christmas party and their festive dresses are no exception – check out their best outfits for some holiday inspiration. Whether you want to copy Kim Kardashian’s ‘diamond’ Christmas dress, or adore Khloe and True’s matching outfits, we’ve got it covered.

The Kardashians are fashion trendsetters and when it comes to their festive outfits, it’s all about the glitz, glam, and drama – even more than usual. The family Christmas Eve party has been a tradition since 1978 and it’s become a ritual for fans to check their chic ensembles yearly.

Best Kardashian Christmas dresses over the years – From Kim to Kylie

Kendall Jenner 2022

View Instagram Post

Kendall Jenner wore a strapless red sequin Valentino gown with her brown hair styled down in waves. She paired it with a nude lip. The fiery red was a favorite for the Christmas Eve party as Khloe and Kris wore the same hue.

Kim Kardashian’s Christmas dress in 2022

View Instagram Post

Silver and grey was the dress code for the Kardashian and West family. Kim Kardashian looked as if she was dripping in diamonds in a silver Versace chainmail Christmas dress with an asymmetrical neckline while her sons, Saint and Psalm, kept it casual in street-style co-ords. North West and Chicago, meanwhile, matched in sequin ensembles.

Khloe Kardashian 2022

View Instagram Post

Khloe Kardashian is the queen of Christmas dresses thanks to her annual matching outfits with True Thompson. The mom of two wore a red gown with a corset top and dramatic train designed by Nicolas Jebran. True was a Khloe mini-me in a custom red dress by La Roxx featuring puff sleeves. She accessorized with a Judith Leiber Santa clutch bag.

We’re expecting Tatum to have a matching outfit in 2023.

Khloe 2021

View Instagram Post

Khloe opted for a Celia Kritharioti see-through silver dress adorned with Swarovski crystals to highlight her hourglass figure. True twinned in a silver sequined Dolce & Gabbana dress with jewel decorations. FYI, True’s designer outfit costs $3395.

Kendall 2021

View Instagram Post

Model Kendall strayed from the usual festive colors and kept it chic and sleek in a black Carolina Herrera gown. The mermaid tail provided enough drama for the holidays while highlighting her lengthy limbs.

Khloe 2019

View Instagram Post

Khloe features on our list once again thanks to her golden dress – she might as well be the angel on top of our Christmas tree. The then-35-year-old wore a one-shoulder gown with a plunging neckline and thigh-high front slit, paired with gold strappy stilettos. True looked adorable in her poofy princess dress but she kept it casual with her shoe of choice: Timberlands.

Kylie 2019

View Instagram Post

Kylie Jenner and Stormi were in the running for best mother-daughter Xmas outfits in their emerald green Ralph & Russo dresses. The makeup entrepreneur oozed glamour in the off-shoulder satin gown with a thigh slit and an oversized bow at the waist. But it was her dazzling necklace that stole the show. Just look at those gems!

Khloe 2018

View Instagram Post

Khloe and True decided on a winter wonderland theme when choosing their snowy white dresses. Koko wore a tutu skirt with a high-low hem and a bejeweled crop top. True looked like a fluffy snowball as the layers of tulle overwhelmed her body but the matching turban hat scored enough points to her on our list.

Although Kim Kardashian’s Christmas dress didn’t win this year, 2018 marked the first time Kim and Ye, formerly Kanye West, hosted the family party. It didn’t disappoint. Unfortunately, the rapper was a little dejected when Kim refused to install an ice rink in their home because it would ruin their landscaping and it was way over budget.

Kendall 2016

View Instagram Post

In 2016, Kendall kept it flirty in a mini black and gold sequin tube dress by David Koma. She paired it with lace-up PVC heels so we can only imagine the foot sweat as she danced the night away.
