Kardashian fans shocked after spotting Scott Disick's 'NORMAL' fridge in his $6M LA home's kitchen

KARDASHIAN fans can't believe that Scott Disick has a pretty standard fridge inside of his $6M Los Angeles home's kitchen.

The famous family is known for their luxurious rooms in their mansions, especially their kitchens.

A TikTok user shared a video of their "favorite scene" of the Hulu series The Kardashians' final episode of the season.

In the clip, Scott's youngest child Reign, seven, is answering questions ranging from "what grade are you in?" to "how old are you?"

However, he is stumped when the person off-screen asks, "what's your IQ?"

"I have no idea what that means," Reign responds, which makes Scott's daughter Penelope, nine, crack up.

However, fans were more focused on where Penelope's was standing: in front of her famous dad's fridge.

Scott's fridge seemed to be filled with generic products and looked quite average when compared to the fridges of other Kardashian members.

One fan wrote: "At least Scott has a normal fridge," as another agreed and added, "Their fridge looks normal."

A commenter explained: "... In Kourtney's house all she has in her fridge is organic foods and Scott's isn't that..."

Scott, 39, shares Reign, Penelope, and his son Mason, 12, with his ex Kourtney Kardashian.

She recently celebrated her love with Travis Barker in a third wedding in Italy- a celebration Scott did not attend.

FANCY FRIDGES

When it comes to Kourtney's fridge, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum is quite the opposite of her baby daddy.

Back in March, the 43-year-old, showcased her fancy kitchen inside of her $9million home.

The modern kitchen has two huge islands, gray cabinetry, and a fridge that included products like coconut water, ginger ale, and organic apple juice.

It's all about health, too, when it comes to Kourtney's mom Kris Jenner's fridge- in fact, fans have labeled it a "work of art."

Shared on Kourtney wellness site Poosh, Kris showed off her three fridges, one which is reserved for ice cream with flavors like vanilla, strawberry, and chocolate.

A second fridge is filled with a variety of green vegetables and fruit and a third is dedicated to dairy.

All three fridges also boasted matte black doors and stainless steel handles.

"I think I will die in peace if I get to have Kris' fridge for one week," a fan joked at the time.

Another added: "You wouldn't understand, I'm obsessed."
