Clothes from Kate Mosss Topshop collections are selling for up to 1k on eBay

CLOTHES from Kate Moss’s noughties’ Topshop collections are selling for up to £1,000 second hand.

The supermodel joined forces with Arcadia owner Sir Philip Green 15 years ago to sell limited edition pieces on the high street.

One eBay seller is offering to courier the model’s silver beaded flapper dress from the United States for £939, describing it as “most wanted and extremely hard to find”.

The item is described as “Good preowned condition, light wear throughout, some loose threads.”

The seller writes “I’m not a company/professional reseller/consignment store and not affiliated with Topshop.

“I’m a vintage and haute couture collector and all items listed are my personal pre-owned belongings.”

A British seller wants £500 for a Mossy hand-embellished cobweb dress, which she says has never been worn.

Other items for sale on eBay and other platforms include the model’s pansy tea dress from 2007 on offer for £150 and a red mini dress for £135.
