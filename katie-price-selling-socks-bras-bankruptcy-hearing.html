Katie Price selling worn socks for 50 and old bras for 200 ahead of bankruptcy hearing

KATIE Price is selling her unwanted clobber for cash ahead of bankruptcy hearing.

The former glamour model, 44 - who is due in court next month - is exchanging her old socks and bras take make a bit of extra money.

Katie has been sharing the items on her Instagram page, kpmemorabilia, which boasts 921 followers.

Taking to her story, the star shared a snap of framed green and red lace bra with the caption: "Individual lingerie framed and personally signed."

The mum-of-five is selling her underwear for £200.

Posting another pic of her holding some pink fluffy socks in a frame, she wrote: "Socks framed and personally signed £50."

They aren't the only items that Katie is trying to flog as she's also been using second-hand website Depop to make extra cash.

Earlier this month she was selling men's designer clothing online, which could potentially belong to her ex Carl Woods, in a bid to increase her funds.

The star is also selling off her dirty, worn designer trainers for £120 a pair.

Katie is facing a hearing on 9 February 2023 at London’s Royal Courts of Justice over her £3.2m bankruptcy debts.

The Celebrity Big Brother winner, once worth £45m, was declared bankrupt in 2019.

She recently opened up about her cash woes in an interview with the Sunday Times.

She suggested that tradespeople were wary of her financial situation.

The model explained: "Because they believe the word ‘bankrupt’, I get judged for it.

"But I don’t let it get to me now; I just think, OK, if you don’t want my business because you believe what you read, then you lose out. Bankruptcy’s just, like, a word. But I don’t let it affect me, because I know the truth."
