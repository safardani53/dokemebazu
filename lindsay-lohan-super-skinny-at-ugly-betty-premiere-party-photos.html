Lindsay Lohan Super Skinny at Ugly Betty Premiere Party (Photos)

Lindsay Lohan graced the red carpet in New York City last night for the Ugly Betty premiere party held at Highbar. She was on hand with the rest of the cast to launch the new season of the popular ABC comedy-drama about the ultra chic fashion magazine Mode.

We’ve got photos of America Ferrera, Vanessa Williams, Becki Newton and Ana Ortiz. All of the ladies were looking great but LiLo, in particular, looks like she has shed some weight and has taken on a super skinny appearance in these photos. Hopefully this isn’t signaling more unhealthy behavior on Lindsay’s part.

Lohan, 22, plays an old school mate of Betty Suarez in upcoming Ugly Betty episodes. She made her show debut on the season 2 finale and has been busy filming new episodes for the start of season 3.

The actress lives with DJ Samantha Ronson and recently completed a movie Labor Pains that is set for 2009 release.
