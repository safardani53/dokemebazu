MAMAMOOs Solar Is Planning To Comeback In The Music Industry

MAMAMOO’s Solar is making a comeback to the music business with new songs and exciting themes. Solar’s management turned to Instagram to provide information on the singer’s solo return. RBW established and managed MAMAMOO, a South Korean female group. The band consists of four members: Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, and Hwasa. Mr. Ambiguous, the girls’ first song, was released on June 8, 2014. Critics praised their debut, crowning them as one of the finest K-pop debuts of 2014.

Fans react to MAMAMOO Solar’s return.

Solar’s return plans were announced to fans on February 22, 2022, by the group’s agency. The news came after the K-pop idol’s 31st birthday, adding to the excitement and prolonging the festivities. RBW posted a sample of a white face on a white backdrop to the group’s official Instagram account. The video depicts dramatic Chinese calligraphy over a white face. Solar will release her mini-album “: FACE” to introduce new songs and make her solo debut after almost two years. The singer’s second mini-album follows the success of her debut solo album, Spit It Out.

The album was launched on April 23, 2020, and caused a commotion on the internet. Solar’s solo debut exuded uniqueness and new notions as the group’s lead singer. Fans quickly rushed to Twitter to express their enthusiasm for Solar’s second solo debut and to wish the idol well. They were also pleased with the agency’s continued attention to the group’s improvement.

Updates on former MAMMAMOO member Wheein

Former MAMAMOO member Wheein has chosen a new agency, which has astonished fans. In August 2021, the brilliant singer used her official Instagram account to inform followers that she had joined a new agency. Wheein joined THE L1VE after various speculative rumors. The K-pop star thanked her fans for their support and waited patiently for her. She concluded her statement by telling supporters that she would give her all in solo and group promotions.
