Mariah Carey &amp; Bryan Tanaka: Pics Hollywood Life

Mariah Carey has always stunned on the red carpet ever since she debuted on the scene! The singer and actress manages to always pull off whatever look she is rocking, regardless of season, fabric, color and more! Mariah has nearly always channeled a classic look with her ensembles, with daring nods to modern styles. She’s appeared at a number of industry events throughout the past few decades including the Grammy Awards, the Academy Awards, film premieres, and so many more. At every single one, Mariah has dazzled, owning her look and her own brand of confidence that is synonymous with who she is as a public figure and artist.

For seven years, she also had another accessory with her on red carpets, her then seemingly inseparable from partner Bryan Tanaka. They seemingly split in 2023, but take a look back at their time together here!
