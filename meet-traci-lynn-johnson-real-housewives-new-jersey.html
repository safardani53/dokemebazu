Who is Traci Lynn Johnson?

TRACI Lynn Johnson is the wife of former NFL running back Tiki Barber.

Barber is famously known for his ten seasons with the New York Giants.

Who is Traci Lynn Johnson?

Lynn Johnson is a former model and the second wife of former NFL running back Barber.

Lynn Johnson and Barber married in 2012, the same year that the NFL star divorced his first wife, Ginny Barber.

The couple has two daughters together: Brooklyn and Teagan.

Aside from modeling, Lynn Johnson has been an intern on NBC’s Today show, where the former NFL star was a commentator.

Is Lynn Johnson joining the cast of Real Housewives of New Jersey?

As exclusively reported by The Sun, Lynn Johnson is in talks to join The Real Housewives Of New Jersey and is already filming with Teresa Giudice and the cast.

Carolina Rauseo and Aikisha Colon have been rumored to also be joining the cast for season 12.

A source exclusively told The Sun that there might be another friend joining the cast for the upcoming season, which has already begun filming.

The insider told us: "Tiki Barber's wife Traci Lynn filmed with the cast at Margaret Josephs' Pride party in Englewood, NJ on Tuesday night.

"The party was shot for season 12, Traci is being tested out to see how she fits in with the other women, she's been filming scenes."

The Sun has reached out to RHONJ producers and Tracy Lynn for comment but didn't receive any at the time of publication.

When does season 12 of RHONJ premiere?

While there is no official release date for the 12th season for RHONJ, filming has already begun.

However a spin-off to the Real Housewives franchise is expected to come out in Summer 2021.

Real Housewives All Stars is expected to give us four to six episodes, depending on the footage.

Bravo, which is owned by NBC, will reportedly air Housewives All Stars on the parent company's streaming service Peacock.

The official summer lineup will include Jersey girl Giudice, 48, and New York socialite Ramona Singer, 64.

Joining them is fellow RHONY star Luann de Lesseps, and Teresa’s RHONJ castmate and sister-in-law, Melissa Gorga.

Real Housewives Of Atlanta stars Kenya Moore, 50, and Cynthia Bailey, 54, also join the ladies.
