This is where to get the metallic gold tattoos seen on Love Island last night

FROM glitter boobs to skimpy bikinis, the Love Island crew are known for their glam looks – and last night it was all about gold tattoos.

Thankfully you don’t need to fork out a fortune to get hold of the temporary glittery tatts, and we’ve found out exactly where the villa hotties got them from.

The Love Islanders wore Rimmel London sticker tattoos, which are available to buy from Superdrug.

Show stars such as Dani, Dr Alex, Wes and Laura decorated their toned bodies from head to toe in the metallic shapes, and they looked great against their golden tans.

You can pick up the exact same ones at the moment, and they currently cost a purse-friendly £5.

Each pack comes with a range of gold shapes and designs, include pineapples, hearts, chokers and even one saying “love”.

  • Rimmel gold transfers, £5 from Superdrug - buy now

The products are exclusive to Superdrug and look perfect for festivals, pool parties or summer events.

According to the website, they are even waterproof, so you don’t have to worry if you will be heading to the beach.

It is easy to apply the tattoos, so you can create your glitzy look within minutes!


HOW TO APPLY SUPERDRUG'S TATTOOS

  • Remove clear sheet and press decorated side onto your skin, facing down.
  • Press wet cloth firmly against the tattoo and hold for 30 seconds.
  • Slowly peel back paper, checking the design has transferred.
  • Pat gently with wet cloth.
  • To remove, wash off with warm water and soap.
