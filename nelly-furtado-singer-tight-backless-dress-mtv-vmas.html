Nelly Furtado, 44, leaves fans stunned as singer looks 'amazing' in tight backless dress at MTV VMAs

NELLY Furtado has turned heads at the MTV VMAs after she appeared in a skin-tight dress.

The 44-year-old singer walked the MTV carpet while wearing a stunning black dress.

It was a floor-length gown, but the back was completely open, and it showed off her toned back.

The only thing keeping her from a major wardrobe malfunction was a thin piece of black fabric across her back.

Nelly was glowing as she posed for photos and walked down the pink carpet to show off her look.

During the show, the singer teamed up with rapper Timbaland, 51, to announce the winner of Song of the Year.

MODEL BEHAVIOUR

In August, Nelly opened up about life in her forties in a rare appearance on a podcast episode.

The early 2000s phenomenon was featured on model Emily Ratajkowski's podcast: High Low with EmRata.

During her appearance, Nelly spoke about the difficulties she faced in her life as a young artist and mother.

She also spoke about what the future holds amid her resurgence into the music industry.

The Maneater hitmaker was full of life as she excitedly started talking about topics like the new music she is currently working on.

Nelly most recently put out an indie album titled The Ride in 2017, and she said she had "always wanted to make" the album.

SINGLE LIFE

In the same interview, Nelly talked about how she's heading into a new, single phase of life, and she wants to make songs that are more upbeat and fun.

The Canadian singer revealed she has been inspired by the music she hears when she goes out to parties.

She's also inspired by her teenage daughter, Nevis, who she got closer to after the two spent time in the music studio together.

Nelly gave birth to her daughter on September 20, 2003, with her now ex-boyfriend Jasper Gahunia.
