Pop star sex confessions - from Cheryl's 'little breaths' to the singer who listens to her own songs

THEY'VE made millions singing some of the world's romantic songs - so it will come as no surprise pop stars know more than a thing or two about love.

Which means they know how to hit the right notes on stage - and in the bedroom, too.

From 'listening to their own music' while making love and revealing intimate details about threesomes, to sharing their own tips and tricks, here we take a closer look at some of the sauciest sex secrets of the world's biggest singing sensations...

Cheryl's little breaths

"I think more men prefer to hear little breaths rather than big loud screams. Otherwise you sound like a porn film."

Mariah Carey's musical tones

She has one of the world's most beautiful voices, so it would hardly be surprising if Mariah Carey's now ex-husband Nick Cannon's revelation that they listened to her songs while intimate were true.

"Can you imagine having sex with Mariah Carey while Mariah Carey is playing in the back? Sorry, Mariah. I said I wasn't going to say that again."

Mariah once said: "I’ve only been with five people in my life, so I’m kind of a prude, honestly, compared to most others in the field."

Robbie Williams' many notches

He's happily married and settled down with wife Ayda Field now, but before Robbie Williams had many high-profile romances over the years.

The 49-year-old was lucky enough to date a whole host of famous women including Melanie C, Geri Horner and Nicole Appleton. 

Both Robbie and Ayda have always been open about their sex life since they wed in 2010, or lack of it - with the Rock DJ singer saying: "Everyone knows there is no sex after marriage. That’s just the way it is."

However, before that he openly admitted he was "addicted to having sex with strangers" and reckons he's bedded more than 110 women.

Liam Payne's sexy lyrics

Former One Direction star Liam Payne, 30, likes sex so much - he can't help but sing about it, apparently.

Speaking to Gaz Drinkwater on Total Access in 2018, Liam explained: "I like sex.

"So what better way than to musically express myself as a young man growing up.

"I’m into it. I like it. It’s good."

His solo hit single Bedroom Floor, for example, referenced his bedroom antics - rumoured to be referring to ex Cheryl - with the lyrics: "You said it was over, but your clothes say different on my bedroom floor. We always on and off until you’re on me."

A year later, Liam was rumoured to be enjoying "mind-blowing" romps with supermodel Naomi Campbell.

Justin Bieber's connective sex

"It's always different... I really like doggy-style."
