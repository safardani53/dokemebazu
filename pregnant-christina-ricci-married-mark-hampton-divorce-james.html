Pregnant Christina Ricci marries Mark Hampton after filing for restraining order &amp; getting divorce f

CHRISTINA Ricci married Mark Hampton after announcing she’s pregnant with their first child together in July. 

The Casper actress already shares son Freddie, 7, with estranged husband James Heerdegen - and was granted an emergency protective order after she filed for divorce from him in July 2020. 

The Addams Family actress shocked fans on Saturday by announcing the exciting news on Instagram. 

Christina, 41, shared an adorable selfie with her new husband in matching white button-down shirts. 

They posed in front of a floral wall - as she held her own bouquet - and captioned the post “Mr. and Mrs.” 

She shared another similar snap from the occasion while the hairstylist posted his own that gushed “just married.” 

The big wedding news came two months after the Hollywood child star announced she was pregnant and expecting her second child.

Fans and fellow celebs were thrilled with the news and showed their support in the star’s comment section.

'LIFE KEEPS GETTING BETTER'

In July, Christina announced her pregnancy news on Instagram with an ultrasound photo, as she captioned it: "Life keeps getting better."

According to Daily Mail, Christina and Mark have become close over the past year and have worked on a number of fashion shoots together.

Additionally, the actress has been known to leave flirtatious heart emojis under pictures Mark has shared of them on Instagram.

Christina also hinted at their romance while wishing the hairstylist a happy birthday in July.

While sharing a playful photo of Mark on Instagram, she wrote: "Favorite person, gorgeous human, bringer of all things happy, magical and good. I love you ... this next year is going to be the best one yet."

The announcement comes after Christina was involved in a very public divorce from her now ex-husband James.

The ex pair - who tied the knot in 2013 and divorced last year - are parents to seven-year-old Freddie.

TUMULTUOUS MARRIAGE

Christina filed for a divorce from James back in July 2020, however, what followed was a vicious back-and-forth, as the movie star was also granted an emergency protective order from her first baby daddy.

At the time, Christina said she “feared for her life” when she was “beat” numerous times as her husband “spit on her” in the shocking allegations.

The Casper star would also share various photos of several dark bruises covering her hands and arms.
