Prince Puma: Career

Mann started his career as Ricochet in 2003 at Chaos Pro Wrestling. He competed on the independent circuit before his debut major outing at Insanity Pro Wrestling’s Sacrifice on February 6, 2006.

The independent wrestling promotions he participated in include Dragon Gate Japan Pro-Wrestling, Chikara, Pro-Wrestling Guerilla, and New Japan Pro-Wrestling.

Chikara

The wrestler made his Chikara debut on June 24, 2006, but was forced to leave. However, Puma returned under the new ring name Helios on October 27 with a new haircut, new attire, and a wrestling mask.

He was then part of a trio named “The Golden Trio” with Hallowicked and Delirious, and then a duo with Dorado named “The Future is Now.”

Prince Puma made his debut for Evolve on January 16, 2010, at their debut show Evolve 1: Ibushi vs. Richards.

Afterward, he made his Dragon Gate USA entering the Dragon 2010 on July 24, 2010. On September 11, he won the Open the United Gate Championship with Cima.

Pro Wrestling Guerilla 

Ricochet made his debut for South Californian promotion Pro Wrestling Guerilla on September 5, 2010.

On August 31, 2014, after several returns to the company, he defeated Johnny Gargano and Roderick Strong in the Finals of Los Angeles 2014. He was granted a shot at the PWG World Championship.

However, the defending champion Kyle O’Reilly successfully defended his title on December 12.

Ricochet won the Battle of Los Angeles again on September 3, 2017, and became the first wrestler to do it twice.

New Japan Pro Wrestling 

Ricochet made his NJPW debut on May 22, 2013, in a tag team victory. He then won Best of the Super Juniors in 2014.

Mann also became the number one contender for the IWGP Junior Heavyweight Championship and became the youngest wrestler ever to win the competition.

Likewise, Ricochet formed “Funky Future” with Ryusuke Taguchi under the banner of Taguchi Japan. But in November 2017, Ricochet announced he had wrestled his final match in Japan.

Lucha Underground 

Mann then signed with El Rey network’s new TV series Lucha Underground and started performing under prince Puma’s new ring name and a masked persona.

WWE

Ricochet made his WWE debut at NXT TakeOver in New Orleans, Louisiana, in April 2018. He then won the North American Championship against Adam Cole before losing the title to Jonny Gargano.
