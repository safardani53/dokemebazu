Premium Relationship Books for Couples (Review) for 2023 Hollywood Life Top Picks Hollywood

In today’s fast-paced world, it is not uncommon for people to resort to self-help relationship books to seek professional help. These books may focus on the individuality of the person buying the book, or they may focus on the couple and their relationship to resolve conflicts and enhance the quality of life. 

There is no concrete formula for buying the right couple’s book or guide. What might work for you may not work for another couple. This buying guide will walk you through the importance of a relationship book for couples and the things you should always focus on when buying one. 

Are Relationship Books for Couples Useful?

Self-help books are quite tricky. They do offer a lot of useful guidance at a personal level, but not all books are suitable for every couple. This is because most self-help books are based on the notion that the person buying the book needs to overcome certain flaws to make the relationship work. Since most consumers of relationship guides or self-help books are women, these books may be imparting biased or generalized advice to their readers. 

Instead of relying on self-help books, it is better to go for activity-based books and guides for couples that allow a couple to work together to understand each other. Working together, spending time together, and discovering yourselves together is what brings life back to any relationship. 

Are Relationship Books for Couples Enough?

Relationship guides can be really useful in letting a couple rediscover the spark and learn new things about each other, but on their own, relationship guides are not enough. If you’re seeking these books as a tool to mend or fix your relationship, then you may need to opt for professional help before anything else. Relationship books can be a great secondary device for fixing a relationship, but they seldom work without counseling. 

What To Look For in a Relationship Book for Couples

Expert or licensed author

This is a crucial step in choosing an effective relationship guide. It is important to ensure that you research the author of the book, and check to see if they’re skilled or experienced in the particular field that you need help with. If you require marriage guidance, a family counselor should be your go-to. If you’re a young adult in a fresh relationship, you may want to consider books by authors experienced in that particular area. 

You should never opt for a book written by a non-certified individual, as they do not have any jurisdiction over this field and have next to no professional knowledge of relationships.

Effective techniques

You don’t want a relationship book for couples that keeps lecturing you on what to do. Instead, you will want a guide that will give you exercises and techniques to fix your problems or work on them. Be it incentivized quizzes or keeping scores of activities done together, effective techniques like these are better suited for couples. 

Practicality

If you don't practically apply all the knowledge a guide gives you, it's of no use to you. Keep this in mind when buying a relationship book for couples. Books that impart wisdom in the form of practical tips or activities are far more likely to succeed in bringing individuals together. Unrealistic expectations within a guide can do more harm than good to the relationship.
