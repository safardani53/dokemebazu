RICHARD MADELEY has been called the 'real-life Alan Partridge' - and his constant string of Good Morning Britain gaffes certainly haven't helped his case.

The presenter, 67, often dubbed "the king of cringe", got himself into hot water again this week after commenting on Laura Tobin's weight in front of the nation.

The presenter told the stunned weatherwoman live on air: “You’ve put on a lot of weight with the lack of exercise, haven’t you?... joke.”

Co-host Susanna Reid was quick to interject, saying: "Well you wouldn't be the only one, Laura, because I ate for Britain."

And it didn't go unnoticed among furious viewers, with one ranting: "Richard literally has no tact he was so rude to Laura and encouraging Lorraine to drink when she just said she was doing dry January he really needs to go cos he's offensive."

However, over the years, others have defended the TV stalwart, feeling he brings a much-needed giggle to stuffy news formats.
