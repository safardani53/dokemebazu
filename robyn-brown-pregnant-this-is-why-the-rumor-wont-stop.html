Robyn Brown Pregnant? Why This Sister Wives Rumor Just Won't Stop

Is she or isn’t she?

It’s the question that keeps being bantered about within the Sister Wives universe — and, no, for once we’re not referring to whether or not Meri Brown is on her way out.

Instead, we’re referring to the subjectofo Robyn Brown’s womb…

… and whether there’s a baby growing inside of it.

It’s true; not the rumor, necessarily, but the fact that speculation over whether Robyn is pregnant has persisted for several months now.

Heck, as far back as March of 2019, Robyn’s former close friend, Kendra Pollard-Parra, spoke to In Touch Weekly about all the questions circulating among Sister Wives fans in regard to this topic.

“No, Robyn is not having another baby," Kendra told the tabloid nearly a year ago, adding:

"She had a really hard pregnancy the last time around and she gained a ton of weight. She already has breathing issues so she has a really hard time losing weight."

Kody and Robyn share two children: a son named Solomon and a daughter named Ariella.

She has three kids with her first husband, son David Preston (now Dayton) and daughters Aurora Alice and Breanna Rose.

In September of 2014, Kody legally divorced his first wife, Meri, and in December of that same year, he legally married Robyn so that he could adopt David, Aurora and Breanna. 

On one hand, it was a sweet gesture.

On the other, it left a bitter feeling between Kody and Meri that exists to this day.

All this doesn’t answer to question of WHY so many people out there continually think Robyn is pregnant.

Over the past few days, ever since Sister Wives premiered on Sunday, fans have been speculating going back and forth on Twitter about Robyn’s alleged pregnancy.

“#Robyn is pregnant, right? Rumors most of 2019 and then this. #sisterwives,” one fan wrote, adding a screengrab of Robyn from the episode where it seemed like she had a baby bump.

“Am I the only one seeing a pregnant belly on Robyn?! #SisterWives,” another fan Tweeted.

So, again, we must ask: Why the heck do people keep rattling on about this?!?

As Kendra explained, it’s because they think they see a baby bump on Robyn on the show.

“Unfortunately, the fans keep speculating that she’s pregnant because they see a lot of old clips of her but she’s actually worked really hard on her figure and her diet,” Kendra said.

“If she were to get pregnant, it wouldn’t be planned. She’s literally not planning any more kids.”

This really does seem to track.

In the weeks leading up to the premiere, many fans on Facebook shared pictures of the cast out of excitement — and many of these pictures were old.

At least one prominent one really did feature Robyn with a baby bump. So we understand the confusion.

But here’s what Robyn told Us Weekly in early 2019:

“At this point, we feel like we’re done, but we’re open to God’s inspiration. When we grow our family, it’s sort of by inspiration. If we were blessed with more children, of course, I’d be totally open to that.”
