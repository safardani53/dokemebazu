A Star Wars Story Film Premiere Red Carpet Pics Hollywood Life

‘Rogue One: A Star Wars Story’ is different from the films in the franchise you know and love. Set before Luke, Leia, Han, and Chewie join forces to defeat Darth Vader, the film focuses on a woman named Jyn Erso (Felicity Jones), who sets out to steal the Death Star’s floor plans. With this map in their possession, the Rebels can conquer the Empire. ‘Rogue One’ features a star-studded cast, who all hit the red carpet for the London premiere on December 13, 2016. 

The Rogue One cast posed together — from left to right, heres actors Ben Mendelsohn, Forest Whitaker, Riz Ahmed, Felicity Jones, Diego Luna, Donnie Yen, Mads Mikkelsen, and Alan Tudyk.
