True Blood Star Splits From Jacob Fishel

True Blood star Rutina Wesley is ending her marriage of almost a decade to Jacob Fishel, according to the papers filed in L.A. County Superior Court.

Citing irreconcilable differences, Wesley, who wed Fishel in June 2005, asks in the documents that a judge deny her husband any spousal support.

The pair has been separated since July. They have no children.

The landscape of the HBO show will no doubt be much different in True Blood Season 7. Now her personal life appears to be in transition as well.

 

Back in 2010, Wesley gushed about Fishel.

She told Essence in an interview:

“We thought, ‘If we could spend four years together at this crazy school, then we can make it the rest of our lives.’ He’s an amazing actor, cook and husband.”

“I enjoy him immensely.”
