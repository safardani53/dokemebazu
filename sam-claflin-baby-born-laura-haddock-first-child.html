Laura Haddock &amp; Actor Welcome First Child Together Hollywood Life

Laura mysteriously showed up to the Critics’ Choice Awards on Jan. 17 without a baby bump — can we talk about how fab she looked, though?! Of course, she was much more slimmed-down than when we saw her rockin’ a huge belly back in November, and her rep confirmed on Jan. 18 that the couple had welcomed their baby just before the end of 2015.

The British couple — who married in July 2013 after two years of dating — are obviously over the moon about their next chapter, although the 30-year-old did seem a little shy about flaunting her post-baby bod on the carpet Sunday night. You look so great, girl!

Sam and Laura announced the happy news with a bump surprise on the red carpet  at The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 2 premiere in London on Nov. 5. The stunning actress was wearing a tight-fitting bedazzled dress which showcased her growing baby bump at the time. Sam was literally glowing as he posed for pics with his leading lady. Sadly, they were there to say goodbye to The Hunger Games franchise. The actor shared a heartwarming snap of himself smiling alongside his co-stars. So bittersweet.
