Who is Shannon Beador's ex-husband, David Beador?

Born on December 6, 1965, David Beador is a construction entrepreneur and reality TV star who is known for appearing with his ex-wife Shannon in The Real Housewives of Orange County.

David is the founder and owner of Beador Construction, located in Corona, California.

Beador Construction was founded by David and his father in 1996.

The company annually generates a reported $13million in revenue.
