Snooki: The Situation is &quot;Already Broke&quot;

She’s a pint-sized, leopard print-loving Guidette, but this Jersey girl has got plans.

In an interview with GQ magazine online, Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi said, “Jersey Shore is going to end soon.” What are her post-shore plans?

Snooki, 23, intends to return to the community college in New York she attended before getting cast on MTV’s reality hit. “It’s small, they have a great vet tech program and it’s cheap,” the infamous partygirl said.
