Sugar Pine 7 is an American comedy and theater production company. The company was founded in 2017 by Steven Suptic, Clayton “Cib” James and James DeAngelis; together, they run an original YouTube channel based on a series of vlogs created by Suptic. The channel hosted a fictitious web series called Alternative Lifestyles, in which three men played top editions of themselves. As of October 2018, the channel had over one million subscribers and over 114 million video views. In January 2018, they joined the multichannel network of Rooster Teeth. At the end of May 2019, their relationship with Rooster Teeth was announced to be over. On June 2, 2019, Sugar Pine 7 released a video titled “Sugar Pine 7 Canceled” and their regular uploads ended, with the exception of their weekly podcast.
