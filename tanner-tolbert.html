Tanner Tolbert Hollywood Life

Bio:
Tanner Tolbert (born Tanner Nathan Tolbert in Kansas City, MO on May 2, 1987) graduated from the University of Kansas with a degree in Sports Management in 2010. He now works at Legends Honda as a finance manager, an is an assistant for the KU football team. Tanner first went on season 11 of The Bachelorette, trying to win the heart of Kaitlyn Bristowe, but only made it about half way through the season. A fan favorite, he was brought to season two of Bachelor In Paradise and immediately connected with Jade Roper.

Best Known For:
Tanner Tolbert is best known for competing for Kaitlyn on The Bachelorette, then starring on season two of Bachelor in Paradise.
