Teen Mom fans rip Chelsea Houska for 'insane' purchase after she shows off huge $225 candle in new v

CHELSEA Houska has been slammed by fans after showing off a wildly expensive home purchase.

The Teen Mom star posted a fall-themed Instagram Reel on Saturday, but her followers quickly noticed her display of wealth.

In the video, Chelsea, 32, filmed herself as she lit a candle in the kitchen of her $750K South Dakota mansion.

On the Reel, she wrote: "When I get to break out my fall candles," and added "my favorite season" in her caption.

However, fans quickly recognized the candle to be from luxury brand Volupsa, with the item selling for $225.

One of the television personality's followers posted a screenshot of the video to Reddit, and wrote: "Chelsea casually lighting a $225 candle."

GETTING PAID

Chelsea's splurging comes after it was revealed that she got a huge pay rise for season nine of Teen Mom 2.

Chelsea and her husband, Cole DeBoer, 35, were sued by consulting company Envy for $3million in 2020 for allegedly withholding money they made from social media promotions.

The U.S. Sun exclusively exposed never-before-seen text messages between Envy's Sara Nemerov and Chelsea’s manager, William Gerard Dzombak regarding salary negotiations, as seen in court papers from the case.

In the message screenshots, Envy’s Sara texted William regarding negotiations with MTV: “We r getting PD on ch new deal w MTV right. Since u negotiated.” 

After batting a few messages back and forth, William wrote: “Okay. On the next one we do. This was included in her original deal.

“Season nine is huge money increase and new deal. They are adding a new cast member.”
