The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special Is Set To Be Released On Disney+

The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special, directed by James Gunn, is set to premiere on Disney+ on Friday, November 25, 2022, at 3 a.m. ET. The emphasis of the episode is on the Guardians, who are in a festive spirit as they try to find a distinctive gift for their commander, Peter Quill. The TV special features appearances by MCU faves Chris Pratt, Dave Bautista, and Karen Gillan, among many others. James Gunn wrote and directed the Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special.

The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special on Disney+ is certain to be a holiday classic.

On October 26, 2022, Marvel published the official trailer for The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special, which gives a glimpse inside the film’s wonderfully chaotic and amusing universe filled with interesting characters. The Guardians discuss how sad Peter has been since Gamora’s departure in the trailer, and how they need to come up with a wonderful Christmas gift for him.

Following that, they go to star Kevin Bacon’s home to abduct him as their “Christmas gift,” which leads to a hilarious incident. Overall, the teaser offers a lighthearted tone that fans of the renowned series will like. According to Marvel, the official summary of the planned TV special is as follows:

“In the Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special, the Guardians, on a quest to make Quill’s Christmas special, go to Earth in pursuit of the ultimate gift.”

Aside from the unusual plot, one of the special’s big attractions is Kevin Bacon’s presence. Fans should anticipate a very enjoyable picture that does full honor to the popular property based on the trailer and storyline.

More information about the cast of The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special

The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday stars Chris Pratt as Peter Quill, who has been saddened following the departure of Gamora, portrayed by Zoe Saldaa. Pratt isn’t shown much in the teaser for the special, but his character is the topic of the Guardians’ conversation as they attempt to get him a good gift for the impending Christmas season and cheer him up. Chris Pratt has previously portrayed Peter Quill in iconic MCU films such as Guardians of the Galaxy, Avengers: Endgame, and many more. Apart from the MCU, Pratt has appeared in films such as Zero Dark Thirty, The Magnificent Seven, and The Tomorrow War, to mention a few.

In important supporting roles with Pratt are Dave Bautista as Drax the Destroyer, Karen Gillan as Nebula, Bradley Cooper as Rocket, Vin Diesel as Groot, and Pom Klementieff as Mantis, among others. James Gunn previously helmed the films Guardians of the Galaxy and Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2. Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 will be published on May 5, 2023, as a sequel to the second film. Gunn previously helmed the critically praised DC film The Suicide Squad, which starred Margot Robbie, Idris Elba, and Sylvester Stallone, among others. Don’t miss The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special, which will air on Disney+ on Friday, November 25, 2022.
