Would you send this to your family? These shameless Christmas cards are so bad they're funny

SENDING a Christmas card is becoming a less common Christmas tradition these days, with many people using social media instead to share their festive cheer.

But for some Brits it remains a time-honoured ritual - which is great, as who doesn't love getting post?

For the truly dedicated card senders, shop-bought often doesn't cut the mustard.

Like Kate and Wills, some opt to make their own, featuring a charming family portrait - and usually a token Christmas jumper or two.

However, if you found one of these Christmas cards on your doormat, you might want to think twice about displaying it proudly in your home.

This gallery of imaginatively wacky creations show some people really do have no boundaries when it comes to festive cards.
