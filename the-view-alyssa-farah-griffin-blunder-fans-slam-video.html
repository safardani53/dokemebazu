THE View fans have taken Alyssa Farah Griffin to task over a name she mispronounced while interviewing Priyanka Chopra Jonas.

During a recent episode of the talk show, Alyssa discussed the movie Love Again, starring Priyanka and a varied cast of actors.

Speaking to Priyanka, Alyssa said: "Let's talk about your new movie Love Again. We all watched it this weekend, it was so much fun."

"Thank you!" Priyanka replied.

Alyssa continued: "I mean you have Sam Heughan co-starring in it, your husband Nick Jonas makes a cameo in it, which is hysterical, but what I didn't know is Celine Dion is such a big character in it."

The pair discussed Celine's acting prowess and continued on with the interview.
