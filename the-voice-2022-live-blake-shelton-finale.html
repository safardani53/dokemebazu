Inside Carson Daly and Blake Shelton’s bromance

It’s no secret that Today anchor Carson, 49, and country singer Blake, 46, are close pals.

Carson and Blake have worked together on The Voice since the show started in 2011, with the former serving as host, and the later serving as a judge.

The two have formed a tight bond across 22 seasons, and they have been the only constant personalities on the show over the last 11 years.

Judge salaries on The Voice

The price tag per judge varies based on their personal fame, their tenure on the show, and the number of wins they have from past seasons.

Most of the judges average around $13million per season.

For example, Blake Shelton is rumored to make $13million per season; however, he’s had the most wins in the show’s history, and he’s been on the show since its inception.

For these reasons, he’s likely to have been given bonuses.

Also, Shelton is a regular on both seasons of The Voice (there are two per year), which means he makes about $26million per year from the show.

Returning judge John Legend also makes a reported $13million per season.

Three new coaches revealed

Three new coaches are set to join The Voice’s panel for Blake Shelton’s last season in the singing competition.

NBC shared in a statement in October that Season 23 will be Blake‘s last, and new faces will occupy the three other red chairs.

Chance the Rapper and former One Direction member Niall Horan will replace current judges John Legend and Camila Cabello.

Kelly Clarkson will also return to her post as judge, swapping out Blake’s wife, Gwen Stefani.

Carson Daly will continue his reign as host of the show.

Auditions are open

Auditions for The Voice are open now.

You can apply now to perform on the iconic stage in front of next season’s judges – Kelly Clarkson, Niall Horan, Chance the Rapper, and Blake Shelton.
