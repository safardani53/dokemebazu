The Voice fans tell off Gwen Stefani over 'infuriating' behavior toward contestants in Season 24 fin

THE Voice fans have called out Gwen Stefani over her behavior toward contestants on the show.

The 54-year-old singer appeared on the final of The Voice on Tuesday where Huntley was crowned the winner after stunning judges John Legend, Niall Horan, Reba McEntire, and Gwen.

The Voice judge was criticized throughout her time on Season 24.

Fans commented on her judging style and outfit choices while on the show.

Most recently, fans have flooded social media with criticism for her input on the final performances.

The contestants battled it out on the episode and the result was announced at the end of the show, with Huntley beating out Lila Forde, Ruby Leigh, Mara Justine, and Jacquie Roar.

Despite Team Gwen being eliminated from the competition during the December 12 episode of the show, she still gave her comments at the final.

Fans of the show flooded X, formerly known as Twitter, with their annoyance at her comments.

Many viewers thought her words lacked originality and that the star seemed to repeat herself a lot.

Several users used the hashtag for the show and gave their thoughts.

FIRED?

This was not the first time Gwen was called out for her judging style.

At the end of November, fans vowed to boycott the show if Gwen wasn't replaced by former coach Kelly Clarkson, 41.

The daytime TV show host was a coach on the show during seasons 14 through 21.

A user wrote on social media: "I hate Gwen Stefani. I miss Kelly Clarkson."

Another agreed: "Me too, I loved Kelly and I kinda don't wanna watch anymore, Gwen's such a disappointment."
