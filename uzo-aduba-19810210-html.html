Uzoamaka Nwanneka Aduba is a Nigerian American actress. She is known for her role as Suzanne "Crazy Eyes" Warren in the Netflix original series Orange Is the New Black, for which she won the Emmy Award for Best Guest Actress in the 2014 comedy series, the Emmy for Women. a 2015 Serial Drama and two Screen Actors Association Awards for Outstanding Performance by an Actress in a Comedy Series in 2014 and 2015. She is one of only two actors to win an Emmy in the comedy and drama categories for the same, the other being Ed Asner for Lou Grant. Aduba has appeared in films such as American Pastoral, Show Roots, My Little Pony: The Movie, Candy Jar, and Miss Virginia.
