Victoria Beckham fans say only the rich can afford her new Reebok collection featuring 220 j

VICTORIA Beckham’s new workout range with Reebok has been slammed by fans for being too expensive.

The fashion designer launched a new sporty collection with 48 pieces covering everything from socks to gym bags.

But people have criticised the new line for being too expensive, with prices ranging from £24.95 for socks to £349.95 for a bomber jacket.

Announcing the range, Reebok’s website said the collaboration: “Mixed the relaxed demeanour of elevated streetwear with technical performance, underpinned by the minimal sensibility intrinsic to Victoria Beckham’s aesthetic.

“The Reebok x Victoria Beckham partnership offers high performance pieces imbued with Victoria’s minimal style.

“These interchangeable pieces move with you from the street to the gym and back again.”

Posing in some pieces from her collection on Instagram, the mum-of-four said she was “so excited” for the launch.

But people have questioned the prices of some items, with jogging bottoms costing £219.95, and even a pair of socks on sale for £34.95.

Racking up thousands of comments, one person said: “Lovely but only the rich can afford your overpriced clothing.”

Another said: “Joggers are like £220…. The trainers even more than that.

“I’d have to remortgage my house to afford the whole outfit she is wearing.”

And while others thought the collection looked good, they still thought it was overpriced.

This person said: “The collection looks absolutely fabulous!

“A tad out of my price range but definitely looks awesome.”

While another commented: “Love the whole attire.

“Hopefully I can afford it.”

And this person simply said: “£300 for Reebok.”

The range is still a fraction of the cost of the former Spice Girl’s main fashion line, with dresses usually costing upwards of £1,000.
