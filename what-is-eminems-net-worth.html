What is Eminem's net worth?

MULTI-platinum selling artist Eminem is considered one of the greatest hip-hop artists of all time.

The Detroit native has sold more than 220million records worldwide and has amassed a hefty net worth since he first appeared in the music scene in 1996.

Who is Eminem?

Eminem was born Marshall Bruce Mathers III on October 17, 1972.

He got his big break when he signed with Dr. Dre's Aftermath Records in 1999 and released his second album the Slim Shady LP.

In May 2000, Eminem released his album The Marshall Mathers LP and it promptly sold 1.76million copies in its first week, breaking the US record held by Snoop Dogg's Doggystyle for the fastest-selling hip hop album.

The album also broke Britney Spears' ...Baby One More Time for the fastest-selling solo album.

His rise to superstardom continued with the 2002 release of The Eminem Show, which reached number one on the Billboard charts and sold over 1.32million copies in its first week.

Throughout his music career, Eminem struggled with drug addiction, overdose, and in September 2020 he experienced a home invasion where the intruder said he was there "to kill him".

Since 1996, Eminem has released 11 albums, ten of which have reached number one in the Billboard 200.

What is Eminem's net worth?

Eminem, 49, has a net worth of $230million as of 2022, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Eminem made the majority of his fortune from his record sales, tours, and merchandise and according to the outlet, the 49-year-old makes around $20million a year.

However, when he is actively touring to support his albums that number can reach anywhere between $30-50million a year.

Eminem owns one home in Clinton Township, Michigan, which he purchased in 2020 for $1.4million and has now put it on the market for $3.2million.
