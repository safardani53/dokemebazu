Who are BlackPink?

BlackPink is a South Korean girl group created by YG Entertainment and consist of four singers: Jisoo, Jennie, Rose and Lisa.

BlackPink's debut album, The Album, featured songs Whistle and Boombayah were the group's first number-one entries on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart and the US Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart.

The group's second album, Born Pink, peaked at number one on the Billboard 200 and was the best-selling album of all time by a female act in South Korea, selling more than 2 million copies.

It also set two Guinness World Records as the first album by a Korean girl group to top the Billboard 200 as well as the UK Albums Chart.

Who has Lisa dated?

In August 2023, Lisa and Frederic Arnault, CEO of Tag Heuer, sparked rumours that they were dating.

The rumours began to swirl when the two were spotted having lunch together in Paris, and grew further when fans speculated they were on holiday in Greece together.

Lisa posted some photos of her trip to Greece, after which Frederic's sister-in-law Geraldine Guyot also posted photos from a trip at around the same time.

The pair were later spotted in Los Angeles together at a private airport, but no confirmation of the relationship has been shared by either.
