Who has Karol G dated? Relationship history explained

PREVIOUSLY, Karol G was engaged to fellow reggaeton singer Anuel AA.

However, the two have since called off their engagement and have gone their separate ways.

Who is Karol G dating?

Karol G is single after breaking off her engagement with Anuel AA.

The two were engaged in 2019 before calling it quits in April 2021.

On April 20, 2021, Karol wrote on her Instagram story: "If just accepting it is hard enough, imagine having to do it in front of millions of people...

"For a long time we tried to keep our relationship away from social media in order to protect ourselves.

"We never used our relationship for marketing purposes, not today, not even almost three years ago when we started [dating]."

Anuel stated that the two will forever be great friends and continue to support each other's careers.

The former couple appeared to end their relationship on a friendly note.

Who is Anuel AA?

Anuel AA is a Puerto Rican rapper and singer. His full name is Emmanuel Gazmey Santiago.

He is often associated with the Latin trap music movement and has collaborated with singers including, Daddy Yankee, Ozuna, J Balvin, etc.

Anuel AA released his debut album, Real Hasta la Muerte, in 2018, at the same time he was released from prison in Puerto Rico for gun possession.

Karol G and Anuel AA met for the first time in 2018 on the set of their music video, "Culpables" and confirmed their relationship in January 2019.

They were soon engaged by April 2019.

Who has Karol G dated in the past?

From February 2018 to September 2018, Karol was in a relationship with Bull nene, a Colombian composer.

Back in 2017, there was a rumor of Karol G hooking up with fellow Colombian singer J Balvin but the rumor was never confirmed.

Her longest lasting, serious relationship was with Anuel AA.
