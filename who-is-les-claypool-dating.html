Leslie Edward “Les” Claypool is an American musician, singer, songwriter, composer, author and actor best known as the bassist and lead vocalist of the band Primus. Claypool’s playing style on the electric bass mixes tapping, flamenco-like strumming, whammy bar bends, and slapping.

He auditioned for Metallica as Cliff Burton’s successor but did not make the band. He worked as a carpenter before forming Primus.
