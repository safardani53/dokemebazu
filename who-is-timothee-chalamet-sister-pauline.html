Who is Timothe Chalamet's sister Pauline?

PAULINE Chalamet is more than just Timothée's sister.

The 30-year-old actress has created an impressive name for herself.

Who is Timothée Chalamet's sister Pauline?

Pauline Chalamet was born on January 25, 1992, in New York City, New York.

She is four years older than her brother, Timothée Chalamet.

The French-American actress went to the School of American Ballet when she had aspirations of becoming a ballerina.

She attended Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.

Pauline then received her bachelor's degree in theater and political studies from Bard College.

She is also a director and writer.

What are Pauline Chalamet's acting roles?

When she was seven, Pauline appeared in the soap opera One Life to Live.

She danced in a Broadway adaptation of Shakespeare's A Midsummer Night's Dream at ten years old.

In 2020, she played a small role in The King of Staten Island, a Judd Apatow film starring Pete Davidson.

Her largest role to date has been for The Sex Lives of College Girls, in which she stars as the character Kimberly.

What is The Sex Lives of College Girls?

The Sex Lives of College Girls is an HBO Max series.

Aside from Pauline, Amrit Kaur, Reneé Rapp, and Alyah Chanelle Scott also star in this comedy-drama.

It was created by Mindy Kaling and Justin Noble.

Kaling is also the executive producer.
