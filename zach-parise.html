Who is Zach Parise:

Zach Parise is a famous Hockey Player. He was born on July 28, 1984 and his birthplace is Minneapolis, MN. Zach is also well known as, Left winger who was drafted 17th overall by the New Jersey Devils in 2003 and signed with the Minnesota Wild in 2012. He captained Team USA at the 2014 Olympics.

Zach is originated from United States. He and Ryan Suter signed identical 13-year, $98 million contracts with the Minnesota Wild in 2012.
